This site was created in 2015 by Jordan Fest. The site utilizes Bootstrap v3.3.5 for formatting, such as the navigational bar and dropdown menu.

Useful sites that were used include'colorzilla.com' for the gradient effect seen on the site,'stackoverflow.com' for guidance and code references to aid in developing the site, and'w3schools.com' for general syntax and web code aid.

Additional development was achieved using Adobe Photoshop CC 2015 for the background and website drafting.

Methods used in developing the site included an emphasis on Gestalt's principles of composition and color theory. Additional visual effects were constructed to achieve a concise theme throughout the site. Such effects included sharp contrast of faded colors, chromatic abberation (seen in the hyperlinks), and an abstract appeal to modern design with some creative liberty issued.
